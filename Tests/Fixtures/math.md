# Math rendering

Inline math like $\alpha + \beta = \gamma$ flows with the prose, and so does a
fraction $\frac{a}{b}$ and a root $\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}$.

Currency such as $5 and $10 must not be treated as math.

Superscripts and subscripts: $x^2$, $a_i$, $x^{n+1}$, $a_{i,j}$.

Greek and operators: $\sum_{i=1}^{n} i$, $\int_0^1 f(x)\,dx$, $\nabla \cdot F$.

Display math on its own line:

$$\frac{\partial u}{\partial t} = \alpha \nabla^2 u$$

Nested fraction:

$$\frac{\frac{a}{b}}{c}$$

A wrapper with blackboard letters: $\mathbb{R}^n \subset \mathbb{C}$.
